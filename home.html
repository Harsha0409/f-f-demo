About Us

At Falcon & Flares, we’re more than just riders – we’re a community that loves the open road. Whether you're a seasoned rider or just starting, our club brings people together for fun, adventure, and a love for motorcycles. Join us for rides, events, and the thrill of being on the road. With a focus on safety and friendship, we make every ride memorable. Falcon & Flares – where we ride together and enjoy the journey.
